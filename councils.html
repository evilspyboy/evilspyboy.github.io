Making cities ready for what’s coming next

Cities are changing faster than traditional planning, permitting, and oversight models were designed for.

Without the right foundations, councils are forced to either allow new technologies to appear without oversight, or restrict them entirely because they cannot be managed safely.

These technologies do not wait for approval — cities either gain the ability to guide them safely, or inherit the consequences later.

Future Ready Coordination

Why Coordination Matters

Emerging technologies are beginning to operate alongside residents in streets, parks, airspace, and shared infrastructure. How these technologies behave has a direct impact on safety, amenity, trust, and quality of life.

CityConnect allows councils to shape how these technologies interact with the community — ensuring innovation works with local expectations, values, and conditions.
