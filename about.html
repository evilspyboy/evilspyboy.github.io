About CityConnect

Emerging tech is here.
Councils can be ready.

CityConnect helps councils engage early with emerging technologies already operating in and around cities. It provides a governed, practical path to participate safely—before complexity, cost, and risk compound.

Built by Practitioners

CityConnect is led by practitioners with experience across government, infrastructure, and regulated technology environments. We focus on the operational and governance realities councils face—not theoretical solutions or technology demos.

Core Principles

The principles that guide how we work with councils.

settings_suggest

Operational Complexity

Emerging technologies do not arrive neatly packaged. CityConnect is built around the reality that adoption happens incrementally and must be managed, not resisted.

shield

Security First

Governance, access control, and data stewardship are treated as foundational requirements—supporting responsible participation rather than reactive enforcement.

public

Local Support

Councils gain access to expertise and ongoing platform evolution without needing to design or maintain bespoke solutions internally.

hourglass_top

Long-term Accountability

Designed to evolve alongside technology and regulation, so councils can benefit from improvements without repeated reinvention.

fact_check

Factual & Understated

Clear scope, measurable progress, and real operational outcomes—without hype, pilot theatre, or technology for its own sake.

assignment_turned_in

Procurement Ready

Designed to support staged engagement, pilots, and governance processes—so councils can move forward at their own pace.
