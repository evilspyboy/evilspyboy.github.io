Built for Australian local government operations

A city-run platform for real-time city operations

CityConnect is an operational platform designed for Australian local government to manage, coordinate, and oversee smart city systems in real time.

It provides councils with a single, authoritative operational view — giving them a way to oversee and manage technologies introduced by external operators and vendors, before unmanaged deployment creates safety, compliance, and operational issues.

Why councils need this

New technologies are entering cities faster than existing regulatory, operational, and planning frameworks can adapt.

Without a shared operational platform, councils are often forced to either block new capabilities or manage them through fragmented, manual processes that increase risk and cost.

Efficient

Supports coordinated planning and resource allocation across departments using shared operational data.

Responsive

Council onboarding and enablement

CityConnect engagement focuses on onboarding, configuration, and enablement — not experimental development.

Once installed, the emphasis is on training council teams, integrating approved vendors, and establishing the operational processes needed to manage new technologies safely and consistently.

PHASE 01 cable

Platform setup

Install and configure CityConnect in the agreed environment and confirm baseline connectivity.

PHASE 02 analytics

Council configuration

Set up council-specific settings, map context, and operational parameters required for day-to-day use.

PHASE 03 fact_check

Training & workflows

Train council teams and agree workflows for monitoring, coordination, and operational use.

PHASE 04 check_circle

Vendor onboarding

Onboard approved vendors and operators, confirm integration pathways, and establish operational expectations.

Engagement scope and training requirements are agreed with councils prior to deployment.
