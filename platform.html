Platform Overview

CityConnect platform overview

A council-controlled coordination layer for emerging city technologies — designed to integrate with existing systems, support operational visibility, and scale as new services come online.

Request a technical overview

Design principles

CityConnect is designed to give councils a safe, practical way to coordinate emerging technologies without replacing existing systems. It prioritises council control, integration-first delivery, and clear operational boundaries between the city and external operators.

router

Council control

Councils define the rules, the map context, and what is approved to operate — with clear governance and auditability.

Integration-first

Designed to integrate with existing council systems, approved vendor platforms, and future sensors through a controlled interface.

Clear boundaries
